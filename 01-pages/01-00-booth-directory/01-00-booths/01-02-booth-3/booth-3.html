About Us

Welcome to Mistersweetpp! We're a tiny home bakery that whips up sweet treats every weekend

and delivers them to school on Mondays. What started as a fun hobby—baking

and sharing goodies on social media—quickly turned into a little bakery adventure,

thanks to friends cheering us on. Sweetness awaits! 🍪✨
